About WebDrishti


WebDrishti is a specially made AI based browser extension that detects Dark Patterns from the website and warns the user about the same. It is a browser extension that helps users to identify and avoid dark patterns on e-commerce websites.

We ensure your online shopping experience is safe and secure.
